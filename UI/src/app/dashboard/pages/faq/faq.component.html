What is Copart Finland?

Copart Finland specializes in salvage car auctions. Copart takes the guess work out of finding the perfect vehicle for your next project. Copart provides key information on every vehicle in our inventory to make it easy for members to bid on and win also clean title used vehicles; hassle-free.

We are proudly part of the world’s biggest online vehicle auction, Copart, that operates in North America, South America, the Middle East, and other parts of Europe. Copart sells more than 2.5 million used and salvage vehicles worldwide via online live auctions each year.

Who can buy vehicles from Copart Finland?

Copart Finland Basic and Premium members can bid and buy in Copart Finland auctions. Vehicles are sold online on business days via Copart’s patented VB3 online auction technology.

Where's AVK?

In 2018 Copart, Inc. announced they had acquired Autovahinkokeskus Oy. Shortly after its acquisition, AVK was officially renamed Copart Suomi Oy.

Where does Copart Finland get its vehicles?

We buy vehicles from individual sellers and businesses. We also sell items on behalf of several industries, for example:

  • Insurance companies
  • Fleet operators
  • Car dealerships

Interested in selling your vehicle? Let us know whether you’re a business or individual seller, and get a free quote today!

Is there a Copart location near me?

There are four Copart locations plus more than 20 sublots in Finland to store and resell vehicles. Our locations are in Espoo, Pirkkala, Oulu, and Turku.

Find a location close to you on the Locations page online. View local inventory, quickly enter live auctions, and find contact information for individual Location pages. These pages are especially convenient for those bidding and buying vehicles from a single Copart location, so be sure to bookmark your favorite.

Although the physical location of a vehicle doesn’t matter to some Members, many Copart buyers prefer to bid on vehicles located within their own geographical region. This makes it easier to preview vehicles in person and to arrange transportation of purchased vehicles.
